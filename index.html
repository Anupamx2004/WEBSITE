Department of food and nutrition

This department typically focuses on educating students about food science, nutrition, dietetics, and public health. It prepares students for careers in food technology, nutrition counseling, research, and public health programs by offering undergraduate and postgraduate courses, along with research opportunities in areas related to food and nutrition security, diet planning, food safety, and nutritional needs of different population groups.
